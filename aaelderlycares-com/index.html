ABOUT US

Care and Compassion
are Provided in Bulk

A&A Elderly Care offers quality care for elderly people that promotes Independence alongside professional standard of health care, hygiene, nutrition and companion. We care for both Elderly and recuperating in their respective homes, irrespective of their religious or health condition. Our Geriatric Care Managers are specialists that evaluate and assess each patient to create a customized care plan. Depending on the needs of each family and patient, our Care Managers coordinate all aspects of care for our patients and families Home care is designed to enable aging parents to remain in the comfort of their home. Declining health often calls for additional help with the activities of daily living.

OUR SERVICES

Services We Provide

Personal Care

We assist our service users with daily living activities such as bathing, dressing and incontinence care.

Reliable and Secure Companion

Our professional carers can accompany you to church, mosque, shopping mall, medical appointment, family and friends visiting.

Light Housekeeping

We aid in light house keeping, which includes; washing, meal preparation, errands..

Vital Signs

We keep track of our service user vital Signs. Our staff are trained Nurse and professional Caregivers.

CareGivers Training

We provide intensive care giving training to new and existing registered candidate.

Certification

We provide elderly care certification courses for people who want to migrate abroad through health care route.
